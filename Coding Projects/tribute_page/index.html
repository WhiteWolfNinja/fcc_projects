Pushing the limits of what's possible

Since the early 2000's, as private firms began to move into the space transportation industry, SpaceX has been challenging our perspective on the impossible. With their visionary founder, Elon Musk, at the helm, SpaceX continues to strive toward the next step in human evolution. Following such remarkable success, it is only a matter of time before the goal of becoming a multiplanetary species is attained.

A few of SpaceX's most notable accomplishments:

Pioneered the successful launch of a payload into orbit by a private space transportation company.

First to launch a spacecraft to dock with the International Space Station.

Initiated the implementation of propulsive landing and reuse of an orbital rocket's first stage boosters.

Constructed and flew the world's largest rocket, the Falcon Heavy, with a cost efficiency three times greater and twice the payload capability than the next largest vehicle, the Delta IV.

What we can look forward to in the future:

Interplanetary manned missions

Colonization of Mars

Deeper space exploration
